About Us

We built TraDG Art Gallery to showcase our creation of digital and traditional art. Everyone is capable of making art but only those who persevere can actually be a real artist. TraDG will feature the creators and their arts. If you like TraDG leave a message using the e-mail provided. Feedback is much appreciated!


What is TraDG Art Gallery?

It is a platform where you can share your visual art creations and other people can see it. It is displayed in the gallery tab of the website together with other creators.
