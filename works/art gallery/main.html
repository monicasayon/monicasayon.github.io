What is Traditional Art?

Traditional art is works created by, well, traditional means. Paper and pencil, color pencils, watercolor, etc - things you can feel, things that have texture.

What is Digital Art?


Digital art is defined as art created using tools such as computers and digitally coded information.

What makes an artist a artist?

In much of the world today, an artist is considered to be a person with the talent and the skills to conceptualize and make creative works. Such persons are singled out and prized for their artistic and original ideas
